Your Local Antique Store

Do you have old items you don't want anymore? Do you want to find that perfect vintage item for your house?

Then look for Your Local Antique Store!

We buy, sell, and repair anything from Victorian furniture to 20th Century toys. Our employees are trained to help customers in finding any item they need as well as find out the history of objects they bring in.
