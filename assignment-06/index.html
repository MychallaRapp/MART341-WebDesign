Waffles

[waffles](./images/screenshot-06.jpg)

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the waffle iron. Beat the eggs in a large bowl until fluffy. Add the other ingredients and beat until smooth.
  2. Spray the preheated waffle iron with non-stick spray and pour mix into waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
