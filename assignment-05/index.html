Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Lewis Carroll

In this book, Alice wanders into Wonderland after following a white rabbit. Alice grows smaller and then to the size of a giant as she eats and drinks a potion and then a cake. She starts to cry, which floods the area, and she is swept away after shrinking down again with other animals. After discussing how to get dry, Alice scares the animals away by talking about her cat. She wanders over to the a house, where gets stuck after drinking another potion. Alice shrinks down again and comes across the catepillar. He gives her advice and she meets the Duchess and has a tea party with the Mad Hatter. After storming away from the tea party, she enters the garden where she meets the Red Queen. Alice is then thrown into a trial where the Red Queen is trying to find out who ate her tarts. Alice is found quilty and is attacked by playing guard soldiers. As she tries fighting the cards off, Alice is awoken by her sister who is brushing leaves off of her face.
